OBJECTIVE

Our objective is to create an online platform for students where they get themselves enrolled in different courses according to their field. Also, they can be updated with latest tech news and blogs. Here in this website, students can undergo different learning tests to examine their skills, maintain their public profile and post their concerns on discussion forum. Our aim is to create a single web application that provide solution to student learning under one umbrella.
